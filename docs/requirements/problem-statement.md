# Problem Statement

Status: in progress

## Project identity

- **Project name:** Varangians LMS
- **Document code:** `VLMS`
- **One-line description:** [TBC]
- **Provenance:** fresh .NET rebuild of a previously abandoned Power Platform (SharePoint/Power Apps/Dataverse/Power Automate/Asana) implementation of the same programme. Only the underlying business requirements are carried forward; the prior tooling and design decisions are deliberately not reused.

## Current state

- Progress tracking and records are kept in spreadsheets that go out of date; tracking is informal.
- No curriculum management process: lesson content is not periodically reviewed, and there is no structured way to propose or make incremental improvements to lessons.
- No structured parent communication channel around a student's progress.
- No mechanism to encourage learning/reinforcement at home, between formal sessions.

## Drivers (why build this)

1. **Progress tracking** — reliable, current tracking of each student's lesson completions and rank progression (replacing out-of-date spreadsheets).
2. **Curriculum management process** — a repeatable process to periodically review lessons, propose changes, and make incremental improvements over time (not a one-off content library).
3. **Parent engagement** — keep parents informed of their child's progress.
4. **At-home learning** — encourage learning/reinforcement outside of formal sessions.

## Desired future state / measurable benefits

Success criteria (initial, deliberately modest): the spreadsheet is retired, and safeguarding/consent and progress data is reliably tracked in the system without incident. No specific engagement KPI (e.g. parent login frequency) is being set at this stage.
